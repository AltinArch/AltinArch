Orientation & Atrium

Solar strategies and the atrium as the spatial heart.

Environmental Strategy

Orientation
& Atrium

The building's orientation is calibrated to the path of the sun — the atrium is a solar machine as much as a social space.

The primary tower axes are rotated 14° from the cardinal grid, aligning the main facade with the summer solstice sunset over Vodno. This single decision generates the building's character — the way light enters the atrium, the angle of the loggias, the rhythm of the facade fins.

The central atrium is 18 metres wide and rises the full height of the podium (6 floors). It functions as a bioclimatic lung — drawing warm air upward in summer, capturing solar gain in winter, and providing natural daylight to all internal-facing spaces.
