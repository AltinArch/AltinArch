Details

The beauty of Altan Nexus lives in the precision of its details — where the design intent meets the reality of construction.

Every connection node, every facade junction, every threshold between inside and outside has been designed with the same care given to the overall form. The details are not afterthoughts — they are the architecture.

The facade system uses a unitised curtain wall with integrated stone fin brackets, designed so that each fin can be individually replaced. Thermal bridging is eliminated through continuous insulation and aerogel edge strips at all structural penetrations.
