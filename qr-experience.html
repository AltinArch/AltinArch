Interactive AR Platform

QR Interactive
Experience

Point your phone at the world and Altan Nexus will appear within it — at full scale, in full detail, in real time.

The QR Experience platform links the physical exhibition with a mobile augmented-reality layer. Each QR code in the exhibition unlocks a specific spatial experience: stand in the atrium, look up at the sky bridge, walk the riverfront promenade.

The platform requires no app download — it runs in the browser via WebXR. Compatible with all modern iOS and Android devices.
