City Context

Altan Nexus in the city of Skopje — a dialogue between the new and the historic.

Skopje is a city of contradictions — ancient bazaars and Soviet housing blocks, Baroque statues and modernist towers, a river that both divides and connects. Altan Nexus enters this conversation with humility and ambition simultaneously.

The project does not attempt to dominate the skyline or compete with the historic monuments. Instead, it frames them — creating a new backdrop that makes the historic city more legible, and a new foreground that draws the eye toward the river.

Skopje Skyline — With Altan Nexus
City Model — 1:5000 — Skopje 2027

Historic Bazaar

The project reads as a backdrop to the Čaršija — its muted stone facade reflecting, not competing with, the bazaar's rich texture.

Vardar River

The building marks the river as a civic asset — its riverfront terraces and new pedestrian connections revitalise the city's relationship with water.

New Landmark

Visible from Vodno Mountain and the city approaches, the tower ensemble becomes a new marker for 21st-century Skopje.
