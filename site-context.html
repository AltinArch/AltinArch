Site & Context

The site occupies a pivotal position on the eastern bank of the Vardar River, at the edge of Skopje's historic centre.

The site sits at the convergence of three distinct urban conditions: the dense fabric of the old bazaar to the east, the modernist boulevard grid to the north, and the open floodplain of the Vardar to the west and south. This three-way tension is the site's greatest asset and its primary design challenge.

A detailed environmental analysis — solar path, wind patterns, acoustic zones, views, and pedestrian flows — informed every decision in the placement and orientation of the building mass.

Topography

The site slopes gently toward the river with a 3.2m level change, exploited to create a split-level public podium.

Solar Exposure

South-west facing riverfront aspect provides maximum solar gain in winter and natural cross-ventilation in summer.

Views

Uninterrupted views toward Vodno Mountain to the south and the old bazaar skyline to the east.
