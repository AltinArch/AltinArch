Form, programme, and the spatial proposition of the towers.

Architectural Design

Architecture

Three towers, a shared podium, and a continuous public ground — the architectural proposition of Altan Nexus.

The project comprises three towers of 38, 28, and 22 floors connected by a six-storey public podium. The towers are offset on plan, creating a pinwheel composition that maximises views, ventilation, and solar access for each residential floor.

The facade is articulated through a modular system of deep loggias, horizontal stone fins, and floor-to-ceiling glazing. The rhythm of solids and voids creates a depth and texture that changes appearance throughout the day.
