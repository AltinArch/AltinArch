Architectural Vision

A Landmark
on the Vardar

Altan Nexus is a visionary mixed-use development conceived at the intersection of the Vardar River and the historic core of Skopje. Rising across thirty-eight floors, the project integrates residential, cultural, commercial, and public programmes into a single coherent urban gesture.


The project is presented across twelve distinct areas of architectural research — from initial concept and site analysis through to technical documentation, photorealistic renders, and interactive digital experiences.
