Urban Design

Urban Situation
& Planning

Altan Nexus is not an isolated object — it is a fragment of a continuous city.

The project responds to the Skopje 2050 masterplan by activating the previously underutilised Vardar riverfront. A continuous 400-metre public promenade, a new pedestrian bridge, and a re-naturalised riverbank transform the site from an edge condition into a civic centre.

The urban plan proposes a north–south public spine connecting the old bazaar with the new riverfront park. Altan Nexus sits at the midpoint of this spine, acting as a spatial hinge between the historic and contemporary city.

Masterplan — Vardar Corridor

Public Spine

400m north–south pedestrian axis linking the bazaar, the project podium, and the new riverfront park.

New Bridge

A pedestrian bridge connects the east and west banks of the Vardar, extending the public realm across the river.

Re-naturalised Bank

120,000m² of riparian habitat restored through constructed wetlands, native planting, and accessible terraces.
