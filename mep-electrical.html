Building Systems

MEP
& Electrical

Integrated mechanical, electrical, and plumbing systems designed for a Net Zero operational carbon target.

The building services strategy centres on four principles: passive-first design, renewable energy generation, heat recovery, and smart building management. District heating is supplemented by roof-mounted photovoltaics (2,400 m²) and ground-source heat pumps utilising the river proximity.

Ventilation uses a displacement strategy throughout office and cultural floors, with demand-controlled ventilation in residential units. The atrium provides natural ventilation through automated roof vents.

MEP Zoning Diagram

Energy Strategy

PV panels + GSHP + district heating. Predicted EUI: 68 kWh/m²/yr. BREEAM Excellent targeted.

Water

Greywater recycling for all WC flushing. Rainwater harvesting for landscape irrigation. 40% potable water reduction.

Smart Systems

BMS with occupancy sensing, predictive HVAC, and real-time energy dashboard accessible to all occupants.
